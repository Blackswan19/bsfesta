How to Use the Music Player

  • Menu: On mobile, click the ☰ menu icon to show/hide playlists and upload options.

  • Upload Songs: Click the "↑ Upload Songs" button to select audio files. If duplicates are detected, choose to skip or add them.

  • Create Playlists: Enter a name in the "New playlist name" field and click "+ Create Playlist" to make a new playlist.

  • Add to Playlist: In the song list, use the dropdown next to each song to select a playlist and add the song to it.

  • View Playlists: Click "[>] Load" next to a playlist to view its songs. Click "X Delete" to remove a playlist.

  • Play Songs: Click "> Play" next to a song to start playback. The playing song is highlighted in green.

  • Controls: Click "Open Controls" to show/hide playback controls. Use "<<" (previous), ">" or "||" (play/pause), ">>" (next), "~" (shuffle), "⟲" (repeat current song), bass slider, speed dropdown, and volume slider.

  • Home: Click "⌂ Home" to view all uploaded songs.

  • Progress Bar: Drag the seek bar to jump to a specific part of the song. Current time and duration are shown.

  • Listen offline: You can listen to your music file by downloading the file and listen offline also.
